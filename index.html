  • Project

    January, 2022

    Smart Neonatal Incubator

    Hyperbilirubinemia is observed during the first week of life in approximately 60% in term and 80% in preterm infants. Prematurity (29.7 percent), birth asphyxia and trauma (22.9 percent), and sepsis (19.9 percent) were the leading causes of neonatal deaths in Bangladesh in 2015. To address these issues we developed a prototype smart neonatal incubator ideal for application in rural area. Key features of the prototype are,
    • Non-invasive bilirubin measurement using transcutaneous photometry.
    • Automated UV-light therapy based on bilirubin count.
    • IOT-based vital monitoring.
